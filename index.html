What is a HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is a non-invasive facial treatment designed to cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate the skin. Utilizing a patented Vortex-Fusion technology, the procedure combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration, and antioxidant protection simultaneously. This multi-step process results in improved skin texture and tone, making it a popular choice for individuals of all skin types.

The Step-by-Step Process

1. Cleansing and Exfoliation

The treatment begins with a gentle cleansing and exfoliation of the skin to remove dead skin cells and impurities. This step preps the skin for the subsequent stages, ensuring maximum absorption of the treatment products.

2. Chemical Peel

A mild chemical peel is applied to enhance exfoliation. Unlike traditional peels, HydraFacial’s peel is gentle and designed to be non-irritating, making it suitable for sensitive skin.

3. Extraction

Using the Vortex suction technology, clogged pores and blackheads are extracted painlessly. This step is crucial for deep cleansing and preventing future breakouts.

4. Hydration

The skin is then infused with a custom serum tailored to individual needs, providing intense hydration and essential nutrients. These serums contain antioxidants, peptides, and hyaluronic acid, which help restore the skin’s natural moisture balance.

5. Protection

Finally, a protective layer is applied, often including LED light therapy to further enhance results. This step helps reduce inflammation and promotes healing.
